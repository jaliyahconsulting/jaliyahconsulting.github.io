Já·li·yah

Jáliyah uses freedom singing to apply movement values and lessons from the past to change conditions in the contemporary movement.

Our Work

Jaliyah Consulting seeks to connect and support people in recognizing patterns, challenging, and transforming white supremacist culture. We aim to support committed to building a more just city, state, country and beyond any borders through:

  • Freedom singing
  • Facilitating story circles
  • Training in story circle process
  • Developing grassroots leadership workshops/ trainings
  • Meeting facilitation
